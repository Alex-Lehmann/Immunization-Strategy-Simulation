Both the Pfizer1 and Moderna2 vaccines require a second dose for maximum efficacy in preventing COVID-19. For our modeling, we made a simplifying assumption that both confer full benefit after one dose of the vaccine. Users can account for this assumption by considering the vaccine availability parameter to indicate the number of persons receiving a second dose per week, rather than the raw number of doses per week.
